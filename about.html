ABOUT

Welcome to Fruits Augmented Reality! This website offers an innovative AR app to bring the world of fruits to your fingertips. By utilizing your device's camera, you can interact with iconic characters like watermelons, cherries, strawberries, and much more.

Simply point your camera at a designated image or marker, and watch your fruits come to life in realistic 3D. You can explore the fruits from different angles, learn their names, shapes, and colors in an engaging and memorable way.
