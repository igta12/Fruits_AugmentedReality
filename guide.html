GUIDE

  1. Camera Access: When you launch the app for the first time, it'll ask permission to use your camera. Think of it as giving your phone superpowers to see the unseen world of fruits in 3D!
  2. Download Image Markers: Head over to the "Marker" section within the app. There, you'll find a treasure trove of downloadable images that act like secret keys. These markers unlock the AR magic!
  3. Print or Unleash Your Inner MacGyver: Got a printer? Print those markers and get ready for some fruity action! No printer? No worries! Simply display the markers on another screen. Just make sure they're nice and clear for the best experience.
  4. Point, Shoot, Fruit!: Open the AR view in the app and point your camera at one of the downloaded markers. Boom! Witness the magic unfold as vibrant, 3D fruits burst onto your screen, right there in your environment!
  5. Interact and Explore: Now comes the best part - playtime! Explore the fruits from all angles, learn their names, shapes, and colors in a way that's both fun and unforgettable.
